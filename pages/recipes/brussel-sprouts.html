Chef Jess's Damn Good Sprouts

Guaranteed to make you 5% more good-looking

Description

She didn't have to do this you know. Give us the best brussel sprouts this Nation has ever seen. She could have been in the pool. Or sleeping.
But, Lucky You - She's a true patriot that loves this nation and would never let it languish in subpar brussel sprouts.

You'd better send her flowers

Ingredients

  • Brussel Sprouts(Texan)
  • Olive Oil(from Texas)
  • Feta
  • Texas Flag
  • Salt & Pepper(homegrown)
  • Freedom(from your patriot heart)

Steps

  1. Marinate Brussel Sprouts for 2 hrs in mix of Olive Oil, Freedom, Salt, Freedom, Pepper, and Freedom
  2. Prepare Freedom oven at 350 degrees
  3. In the meantime, Shed tear while raising your Texas Flag to the same height as your american flag. Collect Tear. Salute. Add Tear to Marinade.
  4. Bake Brussel sprouts for 25 minutes while Stars and stripes forever plays on loop
  5. Top with Feta
