Chef Paul's Brilliant Baklava

Holy shit that looks good, huh?

Description

Chef Paul swears this heavenly recipe came to him in a dream from the good lord himself, and ever since we got it, he won't stop going on about "feeding all nations" and "Manna from heaven".

We're not so sure, but it is pretty good.

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Nuts
  • Phylo Dough
  • Butter
  • Holy Water (Blessed by Priest)

Steps

  1. Layer honey, butter, nuts, and phylo dough in a baking pan until the pan is full
  2. Sprinkle with holy water
  3. Bake at 350 for an hour (enough time to watch Jesus Revolution)
  4. Eat while watching the rest of Jesus Revolution
